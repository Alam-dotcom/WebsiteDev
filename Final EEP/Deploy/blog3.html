A Modern, Elegant Awards Banquet

A modern, elegant award banquet with lucite details at The Bridge Building.

Leaders in science and engineering, BMT pvt. ltd. recognised exceptional employees in front of a global audience with a modern, elegant awards banquet at The Bridge Building.The building's industrial vibe was complemented by a contemporary color palette of navy and gray with marble accent, given by bright white florals in marble vases by LMA Designs which added an elegant touch. Hendecagon's production team kept the vibe intimate yet minimal with modern string lighting.

Since top-notch service was crucial, we pulled out all the stops. With a full premium bar, table-side wine service, a specialty bourbon tasting bar and even gluten-free beer! And because we never encourage drinking on an empty stomach, passed hors d'oeuvres were ready for cocktail hour upon arrival. Hearty bites included crispy herbed risotto balls, margarita flatbread, vegetable pasta, brisket tacos, and miniature pancakes and waffles.

Afterwards, plated dinner was served. The meal began with artisan rolls with honey butter and pear and cranberry salads. Entrées included bourbon glazed beans, baseball cut sirloin and rigatoni primavera, ensuring there was something for everyone! And for guests with any room left, Hendecagon's decadent flourless chocolate cake made everyone’s mouth water. Table-side coffee service rounded out the meal as the awards presentation began.



Event Details


  • Date: February 8, 2020

  • Event: Winter Party

  • Location: The Bridge Building

  • Guest Count: 130

  • Photographer: Sara Bill Photography

  • Catering & Bar: The Bridge Building

  • Florist: LMA Designs

  • Rentals: Mayker Creative & Please Be Seated
