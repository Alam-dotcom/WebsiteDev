Luminous Essence Winter Party

Looking back at a fun, stylish and unique Winter Party at The Bridge Building.

We had a blast with this Luminous Essence Winter Party at The Bridge Building. With the help of LMA Designs and Nashville Lighting & Production, we dressed up the main space with Edison bulb lighting and loads of greenery. Geometric centerpieces with sprigs on greenery sat atop each table draped in charcoal velvet linens.
The evening began with cocktails and a fun popcorn station available for snacking. After mixing and mingling, guests enjoyed live music from Big Swing and The Ballroom Blasters. Later, guests enjoyed some late-night bites including fried mac and cheese, Thai meatballs, and mini tostadas.
